Entry essentials

Who thrives at ISMT?

Undergraduate

  • 10+2 / A Levels / Diploma with minimum 60%
  • English proficiency (IELTS 6.0 or ISMT test)
  • Statement of intent + conversation with faculty

Postgraduate

  • Bachelor’s degree with 50% or GPA 2.5+
  • 2+ years professional experience preferred
  • Leadership narrative + updated CV

International

  • Passport, academic transcripts, financial docs
  • IELTS 6.0 (UG) / 6.5 (PG) or equivalent
  • Visa briefings and travel support from our global desk
