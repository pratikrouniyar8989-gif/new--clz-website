Entry essentials

Who thrives at ISMT?

We welcome learners ready to mix analytical thinking with empathy and grit.

UG

Undergraduate entry

  • 10+2 or equivalent (minimum 60%)
  • Competence in Math/English
  • Admissions interview + essay
PG

Postgraduate entry

  • Bachelor’s degree (min 50%)
  • 2+ years professional experience preferred
  • Leadership statement + interview
INT

International applicants

  • IELTS 6.0 (UG) / 6.5 (PG) or equivalent
  • Passport & financial documentation
  • Visa support through our global desk
