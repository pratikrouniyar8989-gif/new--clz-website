About the campus

Built in Kathmandu, calibrated for the world.

Since 2011 we have been translating British curriculum into hands-on, locally relevant learning experiences—so you graduate with both international credibility and regional intuition.

Our story

From two classrooms to a city campus powering global careers.

We opened our doors in Tinkune to make British qualifications accessible without the expense of moving abroad. Today our Sunderland and Pearson validated centre hosts students from 55+ districts.

What drives us

  • Applied learning first

    Studios, labs, advisory boards, and live briefs underpin every module.

  • Mentorship at scale

    Each cohort pairs with faculty coaches, industry mentors, and career strategists.

  • Global standards, local empathy

    We contextualize global frameworks for Nepal’s evolving economy.
