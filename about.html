Our story

Built in Kathmandu, calibrated for the world.

Since 2011 we’ve helped Nepali students earn globally recognised degrees without the noise or rush—just focused instruction, thoughtful mentorship, and measurable outcomes.

Why we exist

ISMT started with a simple idea: bring British quality to Kathmandu while keeping learning understated, practical, and human.

  • Studios, labs, and advisory boards keep modules anchored to industry realities.
  • Mentorship is layered—faculty, counsellors, alumni, and partner companies each play a role.
  • Global frameworks are contextualised for Nepal, so students stay rooted while thinking big.
