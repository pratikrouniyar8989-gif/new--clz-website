Admissions

Simple, supportive steps from enquiry to enrolment.

Dedicated counsellors keep you informed at every stage—no guesswork, no endless paperwork.

Four steps

The path is straightforward.

01 · Application

Submit the online form, academic records, and identification documents.

02 · Aptitude touchpoints

Some programs include a short assessment or portfolio conversation.

03 · Faculty conversation

Meet programme leads and counsellors for a calm interview + document review.

04 · Offer & onboarding

Receive your offer pack, scholarship details, and payment schedule.

Eligibility

Know what to prepare.

Undergraduate

  • 10+2 / A Levels / Diploma with minimum 60%
  • English proficiency (IELTS 6.0 or ISMT test)
  • Statement of intent + informal interview

Postgraduate

  • Bachelor’s degree with minimum 50% / GPA 2.5+
  • 2+ years of relevant experience preferred
  • Leadership statement + updated CV

International

  • Passport + financial documents
  • IELTS 6.0 (UG) / 6.5 (PG) or equivalent
  • Visa briefings from our global desk

Scholarships & financial aid

Making quality education accessible.

We offer merit-based scholarships and flexible payment plans to support deserving students.

Merit Scholarship

Up to 50% tuition waiver for students with outstanding academic records.

  • GPA 3.5+ or 80%+ marks
  • Application essay required
Renewable annually

Need-Based Aid

Financial assistance for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.

  • Up to 30% reduction
  • Income verification needed
Limited seats

Payment Plans

Flexible installment options to spread tuition costs across semesters.

  • Monthly or quarterly payments
  • Zero interest options
Available to all

Frequently asked

Quick answers to common questions.

Can I work while studying?

Yes, many students work part-time. Our evening MBA and weekend programs are specifically designed for working professionals.

Are degrees recognized internationally?

Absolutely. All degrees are awarded by University of Sunderland (UK) and recognized globally for further studies and employment.

What's the student-to-faculty ratio?

We maintain a 15:1 ratio to ensure personalized attention and meaningful mentorship for every student.

Do you provide hostel facilities?

We don't have on-campus housing, but we assist students in finding safe, affordable accommodation nearby.

Need help with scholarships or visas?

Admissions mentors are available Monday–Saturday, 9 AM – 6 PM NST.
