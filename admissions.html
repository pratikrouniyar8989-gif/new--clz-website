Four-step pathway

Your journey from application to enrollment.

01

Application & portfolio

Submit the online form, academic records, identity documents, and reflective essay. We respond within 72 hours.

02

Aptitude touchpoints

Certain programs invite you for an online assessment or studio challenge to understand your mindset and skills.

03

Conversation with faculty

Shortlisted candidates meet faculty and counsellors for a conversational interview and document review.

04

Offer & onboarding

Admitted students receive the offer pack, scholarship details, and payment schedule with a dedicated onboarding host.

Eligibility grid

Know exactly what to prepare.

Undergraduate applicants

  • 10+2 / A-Levels / equivalent with minimum 60%
  • English proficiency (IELTS 6.0 or internal test)
  • Statement of intent + recommendation
Documents

Transcripts, citizenship/passport copy, 2 photos, resume (optional).

Postgraduate applicants

  • Bachelor’s degree with 50% or GPA 2.5+
  • Minimum 2 years professional experience preferred
  • Leadership narrative + CV
Documents

Transcripts, work letters, passport copy, recommendation, English score (IELTS 6.5) if applicable.
